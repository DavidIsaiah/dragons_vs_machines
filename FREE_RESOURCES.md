## Sound Effects (CC0 / Royalty-Free)

| What | Where |
|---|---|
| General game SFX (impacts, crashes, explosions) | [OpenGameArt CC0 Sound Effects](https://opengameart.org/content/cc0-sound-effects) |
| Large CC0 sound library (categorized) | [OpenGameArt CC0 Sounds Library](https://opengameart.org/content/cc0-sounds-library) |
| High-quality SFX packs (no attribution needed) | [SONNISS GameAudioGDC](https://sonniss.com/gameaudiogdc/) |
| CC0 sound effects collection | [Pixabay Sound Effects](https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/search/cc0/) |
| Game-specific SFX packs | [itch.io CC0 Sound Effects](https://itch.io/game-assets/assets-cc0/tag-sound-effects) |
| Royalty-free SFX | [ZapSplat CC0 Collection](https://www.zapsplat.com/license-type/cc0-1-0-universal/) |

**Sounds needed**: slingshot stretch, bird launch whoosh, impact/crash, wood breaking, stone cracking, ice shatter, pig squeal/pop, victory jingle, defeat sound, UI click.

## Recommendations

1. **Backgrounds**: [Screaming Brain Studios Seamless Sky](https://screamingbrainstudios.itch.io/seamless-sky-backgrounds) - 192 CC0 skies in PNG
2. **Sound Effects**: [OpenGameArt CC0 Sounds Library](https://opengameart.org/content/cc0-sounds-library) - large categorized collection
3. **Particles**: [OpenGameArt Smoke Particles](https://opengameart.org/content/smoke-particle-assets) + [Explosion Atlas](https://opengameart.org/content/explosion-particles-sprite-atlas) - CC0
4. **Particle Editor**: [GDX Particle Editor](https://github.com/libgdx/gdx-particle-editor) for dust puffs and smoke trails
